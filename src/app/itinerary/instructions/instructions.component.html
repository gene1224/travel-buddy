Instructions

  1. You can create itinerary at Itinerary -> Create itinerary
  2. Select Locations, view nearby places, prepare your route on your itinerary.
  3. After selecting locations fill the itinerary details field and save
  4. You will receive an email for a safe link of your itinerary.
  5. Print a copy of your itinerary and bring with you when you travel
  6. You have an option to become a registered user of Baktas Davao to save your itinerary and manage them
